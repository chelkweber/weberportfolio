About Me

My name is Chelsea, or Chelk, and I am a Detroit-based aspiring front-end developer. After graduating from Tulane University with a degree in Environmental Biology, I worked in environmental consulting for a few years. In my free time, I decided to learn how to code. After my first class, I was hooked!

I am currently enrolled in the Front-end Boot Camp at Grand Circus where I am learning HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, AngularJS, Node.js, and AJAX. I look forward to creating fully responsive websites and learning more languages and skills!

When I am not learning to code, I can be found obssessing over pop culture, planning my next adventure or playing with my puppy.
